Birmingham Trivia

  • Did you know that the Jewellery Quarter produces 40 per cent of all jewellery in the UK – the highest concentration in Europe
  • Did you know the Hobbit and Lord of the Rings are based on places and people in Moseley, where the Author JRR Tolkien lived
  • Did you know the first ever hole-in-the heart surgery was carried out in Birmingham’s Children Hospital in 1950
  • Did you know Thomas the Tank Engine was invented in Birmingham by Wilbert Vere Awdry who was reverend at St Nicholas’ Church in Kings Norton.
  • Did you know Birmingham was the birthplace of heavy metal with the likes of Black Sabbath, Judas Priest and lead singer of Led Zeppelin originating from the city.
